Marcus Gapic

Audio Engineer

Marcus is an audio engineer that has been emersed in the audio world for 4 years. The discovery of his passion for audio was plainly the desire to record his acoustic guitar. Through this he learned the process of recording in an extremely barebones software - Audacity.

With a thirst for knowledge in the audio industry, his passion developed further. In his movement towards a more complex DAW, he stumbled across FL Studio.

As curiosity peaked, he attended Metalworks Insitute for 2 years - the Audio Specialist: Professional Sound program. Mastery of audio engineering began to advance at a rapid rate while he graduated towards a more industry-standard DAW such as Pro Tools. He trained under professors with decades of experience and garnered a wealth of knowledge in not only studio engineering but also electronics and live sound and production. His skills extend to Pro Tools, Logic, Bitwig, Reason, Sibelius, and Final Cut.

Thus far, he has built: XLR cables, a large-diaphragm condenser microphone with a C12-styled capsule and a U87-styled transformer, and completely refinished a guitar.

He is always pursuing activity in all audio-based environments - whether it be working as a sound engineer for the Drake Hotel, interning at studios, or providing engineering services at various locations.

Lover of all music styles, he is invariably seeking to provide a consistently professional skill-set to all artists.
