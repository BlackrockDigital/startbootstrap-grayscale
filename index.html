About Us

Weary, bruised and broken?

That’s how life tends to leave us.

But there is life in Jesus — who gives us a New Song — therefore, “Let us sing though fierce temptation threatens hard to bear us down.”

Here at New Song Church we desire to be — and strive alongside one another to actually see — Jesus continue His work of making all things new!

As counter-cultural as it is, we take God at His word that He’s given us a church family so that we can cease striving to pretend that our identity... our meaning... our purpose... be defined by:

our perception of any kind of strength or competence in/of ourselves... our experience of comfort or lack of pain/hurt...

our successes or feelings about how others think of/hope about us...

...

Rather... as those with eyes being trained to see, we begin to embrace that Jesus takes our very real

weariness... bruises... brokenness...

and is both intimately and sovereignly crafting those very struggles and blending them into the very context of His story

to produce in us songs of grace based upon what God thinks of us.

We can be so fickle...!!

But Scripture convinces us that within a community of faith is how we actually grasp

Who we are, Whose we are, and Why we are...

Each of those issues of identity, meaning & purpose are rooted in our relationship with the God who has revealed Himself in the pages of the Bible & the Person of Jesus.

We would love to get to know you and your song... and how our multifaceted songs blend together into a symphony “to the praise of his glorious grace”
