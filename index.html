A Series of Paintings

Home is a series of paintings of Kurdistan region of Iraq. The series of paintings are locations that hold memories for both Mohammed Taha and his Daughter Rojan Taha. Each painting is accompanied by Rojan Taha through audio, as seen through her eye's when she was younger and visited these locations.

Piece 1

 I remember, it was spring, when we were driving, I saw a hill covered by spring flowers. I shouted, look..look at flowers! You parked and I rushed out and ran to the top of the hill where I picked some flowers. I raised my hand with a bunch of flowers and I said loudly..look I got red, pink, purple..flowers. I began to count how many I had.

Piece 2

In the morning, we were driving, it was misty. We got to the mountains. The mist was in the low areas. It was very beautiful. We had breakfast, but we parked, gathered pieces of wood, made tea and had cheese sandwiches.  There were rocks where we sat and looked down to the mountains with the mist between them.

Piece 3

When I was nearly 5 years old, I went to the village Atrush, where we have some relatives. I stayed in the relative’s house for some days. I enjoyed it because they have pets, and chickens. The house seemed big. But recently I went there. It was very hard to recognize the house. The houses were demolished by the regime. After I recognized the house, the remains of the house seemed much smaller than the house that I remembered. The streets also seem to be smaller than that I had in mind when I was 5..

Piece 4

In 1991, when we were fleeing from the attack of the regime, our car stopped working and we had to walk to a village. It was very hard. I walked for a while, and then I got tired. I looked at you and raised my hands to carry me. You put me on your shoulders till we got the village. It was very hard and it took a lot of time.

Piece 5

Hi daddy I remember when we went to Gali Ali Beg, Hamilton Road. There we parked for a while. I went down to the river with you. It was a sunny day. I played with water by the side of the river. The water was a bit cold, but I enjoyed playing with the water, there were also big rocks. Later some other people had parked and their kids came down. We all played together for a while till we started going home.

Piece 6

It was the evening of a snowy day, when you parked by a house of your friend in a village. You said we can’t drive home at night, we don’t have much petrol and there is no petrol station nearby. We stayed with the family. They were friendly. They had kids. We played and laughed very much. In the morning we left after you had gotten some petrol.
