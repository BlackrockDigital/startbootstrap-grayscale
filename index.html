Severus Hama-Owamparo

Community psychologist, author, wellness enthusiast and human rights defender.

Severus' work focuses on intersecting issues affecting gender variant and sexual diverse individuals regionally and around the globe. These experiences, have been pivotal in shaping his vocation and profession.

Currently, he is the co-director of The Taala Foundation: An entity working to support wellness for youth on the margins, through mental health promotion and education in Africa.

ACADEMIC QUALIFICATIONS

Bachelors in Community Psychology from Makerere University.
